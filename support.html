Getting started

Tap the + button to create a project (give it a name, craft, yarn, and needle/hook size). Open the project and add one or more counters for rows, rounds, or pattern repeats. Tap a counter to open full-screen Focus mode for easy counting while you knit or crochet.

How do I track a pattern repeat?

Edit a counter and set a Pattern Repeat (e.g. every 8 rows). The counter then shows “Row 3 of 8” and gives you a little cue each time a repeat completes — great for cables, lace, and colorwork.

I lost count — can I fix the number?

Yes. In Focus mode, tap the big number to type the exact current count.

How does time tracking work?

Open a project and tap Start to time your session. Pause/resume or stop anytime; totals and a session history are saved automatically. The timer pauses when you leave the app so it won’t over-count.

Reminders

Turn on a daily reminder when creating or editing a project. You may be asked to allow notifications the first time.

Subscription, free trial & billing

The app offers a Monthly and a Yearly subscription with a free trial. After the trial it renews automatically unless canceled at least 24 hours before the period ends.

How do I restore my subscription on a new device?

On the paywall, tap Restore Purchases (also available in Settings), signed in with the same Apple Account you subscribed with.

How do I cancel or manage my subscription?

Go to Settings → your name → Subscriptions on your iPhone (or apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions), or tap Manage Subscription in the app’s Settings.

Where is my data stored? Is it backed up?

All your data is stored privately on your device. It’s included in your device/iCloud device backup. Deleting the app removes its data.

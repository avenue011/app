Privacy Policy

Knitting & Crochet Row Counter · Last updated: June 7, 2026

Overview

Knitting & Crochet Row Counter (the “App”) is developed by MD Shahariar Nazim Joy. Your privacy matters. This App is designed to work entirely on your device, and it does not collect, sell, share, or transmit your personal information.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal data. All content you create — projects, counters, stitch counts, notes, photos, time-tracking sessions, and settings — is stored locally on your device. We have no servers and no access to your data.

Photos

If you add a project photo, the App uses the system photo picker to import only the image(s) you choose. Photos are stored locally in the App and are never uploaded.

Notifications

If you enable reminders, they are scheduled as local notifications on your device. No notification data leaves your device.

Purchases & Subscriptions

Subscriptions are processed by Apple through the App Store. We never receive or store your payment details. Apple may process transaction and subscription information in accordance with the Apple Privacy Policy. The App only reads your subscription status from Apple to unlock features.

Analytics & Tracking

The App contains no third-party analytics, no advertising, and no tracking. We do not use cookies or device identifiers for tracking.

Data Sharing

We do not share your information with anyone, because we do not collect it.

Children’s Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone, including children under 13.

Your Control

You can delete any project, counter, photo, or session at any time within the App. Deleting the App from your device removes all locally stored data.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a new “Last updated” date.
